MountArayat

Mount Arayat is a potentially active stratovolcano located in the province of Pampanga on the island of Luzon, Philippines, rising to a height of 1,026 meters. The volcano has a breached crater on its northwest side with a smaller andesitic dome in the collapse amphitheater.
